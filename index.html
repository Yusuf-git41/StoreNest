The Real Struggle

You shouldn't pay for empty space

Students, working pros, and transferable-job employees face the same hidden cost.

Living away from hometown? The rent trap is real.

You rent a room for ₹2500/month while studying or working in a different city. But when you go back home for semester break, long vacation, or temporary work assignment — you can't vacate fully because your belongings (samaan) are still inside. The landlord still demands full rent, even if you are not there. That means you lose money every month just to store your own stuff in an empty room. Shifting everything back home is expensive and impractical. So you're forced to pay dead rent for zero benefit.

Average wasted rent per break: ₹7,500+ for 3 months

    Meet The Founder

    Driven by real problems

    Built from personal experience to solve a universal struggle

    Yusuf Mohammad

    Founder, StoreNest | Mining Engineering Student

    Yusuf Mohammad is a mining engineering student who experienced this problem firsthand. During college vacations when he would return to his hometown, he was forced to pay full rent — ₹2500 per month — just to keep his belongings in a room he wasn't even staying in.

    As a problem solver by nature, Yusuf couldn't accept this as just "how things work." He realized that millions of students and working professionals across India face the exact same struggle every year — paying dead rent for empty rooms during semester breaks, long vacations, or temporary work assignments.

    That's when StoreNest was born. Still pursuing his mining engineering degree, Yusuf built StoreNest to solve his own problem and help countless others. His vision is simple: create a cheap, secure, and trustworthy storage solution that allows people to vacate their rooms completely, stop paying unnecessary rent, and still have their belongings safe at a fraction of the cost.

    Today, as a student entrepreneur, Yusuf is proving that you don't need to wait for graduation to solve real problems. StoreNest continues to grow, helping students and professionals save thousands in dead rent.

    "As a student, every rupee matters. I was paying rent for a room I wasn't sleeping in. That's when I realized — this problem needs a solution, not acceptance."

    - Yusuf Mohammad, Mining Engineering Student & Founder
